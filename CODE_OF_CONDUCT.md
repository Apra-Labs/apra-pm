# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** as its Code of Conduct.

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Full Text

The full Contributor Covenant v2.1 text is available at:
https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at:

**contact@apralabs.com**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter.
